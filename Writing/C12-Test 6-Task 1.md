During a recent plane journey, you sat next to a businessman who owns a chain of restaurants. You talked to him and he suggested that you should contact him about a possible job in one of his restaurants. Write a letter to this businessman. 
In your letter 
• remind him when and where you met 
• tell him what kind of job you are interested in 
• say why you think you would be suitable for the job 
Write at least 150 words. 
You do NOT need to write any addresses. Begin your letter as follows: Dear ..................... ,
