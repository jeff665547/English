A large company in your area has decided to spend a certain amount of money, either to sponsor a local children's sports team for two years, or to pay for two open-air concerts. It has asked for feedback from the general public. Write a letter to the company. 
In your letter 
• describe the benefits of sponsoring the sports team 
• summarise the benefits of paying for the concerts 
• say how you think the company should spend the money 
Write at least 150 words.
